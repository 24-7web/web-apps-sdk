Blog overall layout

Is used by these modules:

  • {module_blog}

Accessible from

  • Admin Console:Â Site Manager > Module Templates >Blog Layouts
  • SFTP & Develop Mode: /Layouts/Booking/overall.html

Renders these tags

{tag_blogtitle}Name of the blog
{tag_blogcategorylist}List of top used categories. Default is top 10 entries. You can customize to display more or less
{tag_blogdescription}Description of the blog
{tag_postlist,number of the posts}Either an individual post or the list of the posts in blog. {tag_postlist,number of the posts} e.g. {tag_postlist,20} which displays 20 posts
{tag_previouspage}Link to the previous page of blog posts
{tag_pagination}Enhanced pagination e.g. 1 .. 2 3 4 5 .. 10 to provide fast access to adjacentpages
{tag_nextpage}Link to the next page of blog posts
{tag_blogrecentpost,number of posts}Lists recent posts in blog. Default is 10 entries. You can customize to displayless/more.
{tag_blogtagcloud,number of tags}Tag cloud for your posts. Default is top 10 entires. You can customize to displayless/more.
{tag_blogtaglist,number of tags}List of top used tags. Default is first 10 entries sorted alphabetically. You can customize to display less/more
{tag_blogpagelist}Displays a list of all the pages in your blog. Provides a link to each page access
{tag_blogcalendar}Calendar to retrieve blog posts by date
