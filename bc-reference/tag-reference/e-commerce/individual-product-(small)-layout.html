Individual product (small) layout

Is used by these modules

  • {module_productfeaturelist}
  • {module_product}
  • {module_productresults}

Accessible from

  • Admin Console: Site Manager > Module Templates > Online Shop Layouts
  • SFTP & Develop Mode: /Layouts/OnlineShop/small_product.html
