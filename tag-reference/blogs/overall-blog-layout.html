Renders these tags


Most common tags used


{tag_blogtitle}Name of the blog
{tag_blogcategorylist}List of top used categories. Default is top 10 entries. You can customize to display more or less
{tag_blogdescription}Description of the blog
{tag_postlist,number of posts}Either an individual post or the list of posts in blog. {tag_postlist,number of posts} e.g. {tag_postlist,20} which displays 20 posts
{tag_previouspage}Link to the previous page of blog posts
{tag_pagination}Enhanced pagination e.g. "1 .. 2 3 4 5 .. 10" to provide fast access to adjacentpages
{tag_nextpage}Link to the next page of blog posts
{tag_blogrecentpost}Lists recent posts in blog. Default is 10 entries. You can customize to displayless/more.
{tag_blogtagcloud}Tag cloud for your posts. Default is top 10 entires. You can customize to displayless/more
{tag_blogtaglist}List of top used tags. Default is first 10 entries sorted alphabetically. You can customize to display less/more

Other tags


{tag_blogpagelist}Displays a list of all the pages in your blog. Provides a link to each page access
{tag_blogcalendar}}Calendar to retrieve blog posts by date
